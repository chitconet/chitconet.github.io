About CHITCO

Chitco was established in 2002, located in XiXiang, Shenzhen, with SMT, injection workshop, metal stamping workshop and 4 assembling lines. The annual production capacity is 600,000 units.It is a modern household appliances manufacture that passed ISO 9001 and with BSCI approval, integrating R&D, manufacturing and sales, specializing in the production of Sous vide circulator, mixers, blenders, choppers and professional steak beefer..

We focus on technological innovation with a number of invention patents, utility model patents and appearance patents. The principle of the company is "No best but better product, better quality better service", based on it we established strong R&D team, quality control system and sales &customer-service system.Our products are exported to Europe, America, Australia and Asia. Especially in the field of sous vide circulators we are the pioneer with developing more than 20 models, and are the first one to be authorized with IPX7 waterproof certificate. We are on the way to continue up grading our production capacity, product range and product quality &service.
